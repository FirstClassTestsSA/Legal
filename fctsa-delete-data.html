Delete Your Data

Firearm Competency Test SA

Last updated: 11 July 2026

In short: This app has no user accounts and no sign-in. The only information we hold about a user is the purchase (entitlement) record for a one-time unlock, kept on our behalf by RevenueCat. You can ask us to delete that record by email and we will do so within 30 days. Google keeps its own record of the transaction under its own policies, which is outside our control.

1. Scope

This page explains how to request deletion of personal information processed by Firearm Competency Test SA (Google Play package za.co.fctsa.firearms), published by First Class Tests SA ("we", "us"). For the purposes of the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA), we are the responsible party for this limited processing.

The app has no user accounts and no login. There is no account to delete. A deletion request therefore concerns the purchase record only.

2. How to request deletion

Send us an email with the details below and we will locate and delete your purchase record.

  1. Email fctsa.apps@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line Data deletion request.
  3. Send it from the Google account you used to buy the unlock, which helps us verify the request.
  4. Include your approximate purchase date and, if you have it, your Google Play order ID (it starts with GPA. and appears in your Google Play order confirmation email or receipt).

Processing time: we will action your request and confirm by reply within 30 days. We do not charge for this.

3. What we delete

On request, we permanently delete the purchase / entitlement record held by RevenueCat that is associated with your purchase. This is the only personal information we process, and it consists of an anonymous, app-generated identifier (not linked to your real-world identity), your purchase and entitlement records, your device's country (from the store), and technical metadata such as app version, SDK version, operating-system version, and device model.

Your study progress, quiz results, and unlock status are stored only on your device — we never receive them. Uninstalling the app removes that on-device data immediately; no request to us is needed.

4. What is kept, and what is outside our control

Google Play (Google) keeps its own record of the transaction — including your payment and order history — and retains it for legal, financial, and tax-compliance reasons. This is Google's retention under Google's policies, not ours, and we cannot delete it on your behalf. You can review the data tied to your Google account, and the controls available to you, at myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy.

When the app checks for content updates it sends transient technical data — your device's IP address, along with the platform, the app's runtime version, the update channel, the current update identifier, and a user-agent string — to Expo solely to deliver the correct update. This is not stored by us as a user profile and is not a record we can delete on your behalf; it is retained, if at all, under Expo's own policy.

For the full picture of what we process and why, see our Privacy Policy.
